Projects

Here are some of the projects I made while learning Unreal.

Borderline



A music video I made while learning Metahuman. I modeled the environment, animated the face, blended body animations, and put it all together in Sequencer.

Night Shift



A previs sequence I created in UE 5. I used Niagara Systems to create fireworks FX, and Sequencer to animate cameras and chararcters.

After Hours



A short I created in UE 4. I used Sequencer to animate cameras, chararcters, and props. I added sound in After Effects.

Strange Encounters



A short I created in UE 4. I used post process volume for lighting FX and Sequencer to animate cameras and chararcters. I added sound and edited in After Effects.

After Hours Pt. 2



A previs sequence for the continuation of the short, "After Hours." I used Sequencer to animate cameras, chararcters, and props.
